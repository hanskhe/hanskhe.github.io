About me

Looking around, I find there are thousands of things to explore, and unlimited potential for learning. That is the core of my life, the urge to understand, and to dive into the unknown. No day is complete if I haven't learned something new.

Having travelled to and lived in the Philippines changed how I view the world, and made me understand the unfairness and differences that prevail in the world. Through influencing the decision makers, and spreading awareness, I hope to do my part in rectifying the wrongs that have been done to millions of people.

I am educated as a civil engineer in computer science, and still find it fascinating what can be done with some simple lines of code. My dream is to use computer science to change the world!
